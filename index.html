The Swami 3


Who are the Swami 3?

The Swami 3 are a band of three brothers: Rohan, Shaan, and Noah. Shaan and Noah are twins aged ten, and Rohan is the eldest aged 12. Visit the "origin" section to learn more about how they became a band.

Rohan

Rohan is the Swami 3's pianist. He keeps the rhythm going with chords and some melody, as well as acting as a substitute for that missing bassist. Everyone enjoys listening to him play.

Shaan

Shaan is the star of the Swami 3. Shining in drums and vocals, he not only plays by far the most important (and difficult) role, but also makes everyone smile the most when playing.

Noah

Noah is the Swami 3's guitarist. He jams with those chords and the occasional solo. Nobody is ever unhappy watching Noah play with that big, wide grin on his face.
