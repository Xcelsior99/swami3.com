Who are the Swami 3?

The Swami 3 are brothers originally from San Francisco, California, who started learning individual instruments according to their personalities at age 5. In 2020, COVID gave them an opportunity to spend more time practicing together, and they also started using music to connect and uplift friends online. Rohan (14) is fluid on the keys, Shaan (12) dazzles with drums & vocals, and Noah (12) jams on guitar. Huge credit to their British teacher, Phil Surtees, who has continued Zoom lessons with them throughout the pandemic. Visit the "background" page to learn more about how they became a band.

Rohan

Rohan, also the coder of this website, is the Swami 3's pianist. He keeps the rhythm going with chords and some melody, as well as acting as a substitute for that missing bassist. Everyone enjoys listening to him play.

Shaan

Shaan is the star of the Swami 3. Shining in drums and vocals, he plays probably the most important role in the band. He loves to please by expressing himself using his acting skills.

Noah

Noah is the Swami 3's guitarist. He jams with those chords and the occasional solo. Nobody is ever unhappy watching Noah play with that big, wide grin on his face.
