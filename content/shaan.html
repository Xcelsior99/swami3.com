My name is Shaan. I am 11 years old and play the drums and sing. I started learning drums and vocals when I was 5, when our parents thought we should learn music and chose instruments for us that fit our personalities. All the singing started when I was sitting in the car, singing along with the song playing from my mom's phone, and she said she would put me in the chorus. At first I was mad and reluctant, but eventually grew to like it. I joined the San Francisco Boys Chorus (SFBC) in January 2016 and was promoted to their Intermediate Collegium level in just 3.5 years. In December 2018 I was selected to sing a solo with the world-famous Chanticleer men's acapella group in a winter concert. I also got the opportunity to perform the National Anthem both at an Oakland A's game and a Golden State Warriors game (you can see that performance here). Finally, I was chosen to sing with other kids in the SF Opera's production of Hansel & Gretel in November 2019. Sadly, I had to leave the SFBC when we moved to Singapore, but I am very excited to have been accepted to the Voices of Singapore Childrens' Choir starting in January 2021. I cannot wait to see what I get to do with them. Meanwhile, I have become very good at singing while drumming, and play an important role in our band.
