Rohan


Rohan started playing the piano at the age of five, and he started out reluctant. Over time, not only did he learn more and more complicated songs, but he also developed an ability to understand and play a song just by listening to it, as well as some partly-formed perfect pitch. He is currently a twelve-year-old seventh grader and is still learning more and more, every day.
