Rohan


Ever since Rohan reluctantly started learning piano at age 5, it has become his greatest passion. He started out following the Faber lesson books - but that only lasted him a few years. Once he finished all five levels of the books and was ready to move on, his teacher started giving him all sorts of new pieces to learn, including classical pieces, recent music, band pieces to play with his brothers (of course), and jazz sheets where he has opportunities to improvise. Rohan has recently developed the ability to play a song by ear, meaning that he can listen to a song a few times and recreate it on the piano. He also has perfect pitch (kind of) and aims to improve that unique ability. He is currently a 14-year-old ninth grader and is continuing to learn more jazz, classical, and band pieces.
